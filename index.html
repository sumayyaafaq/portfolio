About Me

I’m Sumayya — a web developer with a flair for visual storytelling and a deep appreciation for clean, purposeful design.

My work blends front-end development skills with graphic design, allowing me to craft both functional websites and beautiful brand visuals. I code in multiple languages, create brand identities, and build digital experiences that feel intentional and human.

My background is as colorful as my palette — with roots in religious education, a stint as a school radio jockey (thanks to my multilingual edge!), and a passion for making learning and tech more accessible. I'm especially driven by projects that support education, creativity, and women-led businesses.

Let’s create something meaningful — whether it’s a brand, a website, or your next big idea.
